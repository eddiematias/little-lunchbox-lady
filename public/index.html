How To Order

I post my menu for the week every Sunday, you can preorder your lunchboxes for the week or order the day of before 10am. Lunches are $10 each, including tax and delivery fee. 5 lunchbox minimum for deliveries. You can pay however is convenient for you- phone, text or email. I accept all forms of payment as well. Everyday I have a homemade meal you can order. I try and accommodate all diets and lifestyles so you'll notice my menu is very diverse. That's just how I like it. :)

Monday - BLTs (or ALTs) & Citrus Herb Salad

Is there anything in the whole world that is really better than a sandwich full of thick bacon, a fat tomato, crispy lettuce and a lil mayo? It's a tough question. Of course for my herbivores out there I have not forgotten you! ALT's are avocado, lettuce and tomato- and they might be the second best thing. ;) Served with a small citrus & herb salad on a bed of spicy arugula with house made blueberry vinaigrette. *Add avocado on your BLT for $1 upcharge! *Vegetarian Option Available

Tuesday - Caritas Burrito Box

Um, step aside Chipotles! My Burritos this week are stuffed with pulled pork, corn jalapeño salsa, cilantro lime rice, red onions, black beans and cheddar cheese. Served with a guacamole salad and lime wedge. *Vegetarian Option Available

Wednesday - Tuna Melt Sandwich & Artichoke Soup

So remember that Green Goddess Sandwich I made a few weeks back? Yeah, of course you do! That was the base for this little creation. Green Goddess aioli, sprouts, cucumbers, butter lettuce and avocado with a generous scoop of seasoned chunky Albacore tuna fish. I think it's just what this weather calls for. Served up with my signature spicy Artichoke soup.

Throwback Thursday - Southern Cobb Salad

​Mmmmmm, everything I love about the South! Rotisserie chicken, roasted corn, pecans, avocado, hard boiled eggs, heirloom tomatoes, bacon and blue cheese. Served up with house made red wine vinaigrette. Served with spicy "fire" crackers. . *Gluten Free without Crackers *Contains nuts!

Friday - Thai Chicken Wraps & Cucumber Salad

Crunchy and flavorful, this is the perfect fast meal to gobble down during your hectic Friday work lunch rush. Filled with chicken, peanuts, cilantro, carrots, snap peas and my ah-mazzziiiing peanut sauce for dipping spreading, eating with a spoon- enjoying however you please. This is served with Thai cucumber salad. *Contains nuts!
