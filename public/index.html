How To Order

I post my menu for the week every Sunday, you can preorder your lunchboxes for the week or order the day of before 10am. Lunches are $10 each, including tax and delivery fee. ($2 charge for single orders- lunches are better when eaten together!) Order however is convenient for you- phone, text or email. I accept all forms of payment as well. Everyday I have a homemade meal you can order. I try and accommodate all diets and lifestyles so you'll notice my menu is very diverse. That's just how I like it. :)
